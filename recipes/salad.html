Salad

Description

This really is the best homemade Caesar salad! With romaine lettuce, Parmesan cheese, homemade croutons and a simple creamy homemade dressing.I believe I have unlocked the best homemade Caesar salad recipe. It is fairly authentic. but still quick, easy, and approachable. Make this salad a part of your regular salad routine at home, I know you are going to love it!

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups torn bread cubes
  • Mayonnaise
  • Anchovy paste
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Fresh minced garlic
  • Dijon mustard
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

Make the Croutons

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Drizzle the bread cubes with olive oil and salt
  3. Spread on to a pan and bake 7-10 minutes until edges turn brown

Make the dressing

  1. Whisk all ingredients together
  2. Store in fridge

Assemble the salad

  1. In a large bowl toss the lettuce with desired amount of dressing
  2. Add half of the croutons and toss again
  3. Add remainging croutons and dressing and serve immediately
